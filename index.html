Why Sepia

Memories worth keeping,
from every angle.

Presence

No screens at the party.

Photos stay hidden until your chosen reveal time, so nobody is scrolling a feed mid-moment. Everyone stays in the room, in the night, in the day, present.

Perspective

Every guest is a photographer.

One camera sees one angle. Sepia pools everyone's shots into a single film, so you get the moments you never saw: the candid, the off-angle, the unrepeatable.

Scarcity

A limited roll makes better photos.

Just like a real disposable camera, a capped number of shots makes people think before they press. Fewer frames, more meaning, no hundreds of near-duplicates.

Together

One film, developed as one.

When the event ends, the whole album unlocks at once: a single shared "develop" moment, then a permanent film you can revisit, share and download forever.
